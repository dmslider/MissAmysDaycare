About Miss Amy

A trusted name in Surrey Quays childcare

Over a decade of experience. Deep commitment to every child's individual journey.

Miss Amy's Daycare is a professional, Ofsted-registered early years setting in Surrey Quays, SE16. Miss Amy provides a safe, stimulating and deeply nurturing environment where every child is seen, valued and supported.

Rooted in the EYFS framework, with specialist experience supporting children with additional needs — including non-verbal and limited speech — Miss Amy's approach is always personal, always intentional.

Her long-term vision is to grow from a highly regarded childminding setting into a fully registered early years prep school — raising the standard of early education in the community.
