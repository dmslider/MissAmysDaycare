About Miss Amy

A trusted name in Surrey Quays childcare

Miss Amy's Daycare is a professional, Ofsted-registered early years setting in Surrey Quays, SE16. With over a decade of experience, Miss Amy provides a safe, stimulating and nurturing environment where every child is seen, valued and supported.

Rooted in the EYFS framework, with specialist experience supporting children with additional needs — including non-verbal and limited speech children.
